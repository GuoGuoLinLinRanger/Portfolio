Flagship · Autonomous Systems

Osmia

An LLM that researches and trades crypto, alone — and proves it.
ProblemI wanted to know whether an LLM could research and trade unattended for months — and I wanted to prove the result, not claim it.
ApproachSeven systemd services on a Linux box at home, 24/7: ~288 ingest runs and ~144 reasoning cycles a day across 14 assets. Every call is scored against what actually happened, and the misses feed the next cycle. A self-built 4-cohort Fear & Greed index drives an adaptive risk layer.
Result4+ months unattended, zero pipeline stalls. 64% directional across 380+ scored verdicts — 14 points over a coin-flip, with stops that keep the losers small. The verdict log is right here; the exported CSV is public.
