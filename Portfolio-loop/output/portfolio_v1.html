What I build

Flagship · Autonomous Trading

Osmia

An LLM that researches and trades crypto — alone.

Seven systemd services on a Linux box at home, running 24/7 for four-plus months unattended. It does ~288 ingest runs and ~144 reasoning cycles a day across 14 assets, scores every verdict against reality, and learns from the misses. I built a 4-cohort synthetic Fear & Greed index from scratch and layered F&G-adaptive stop-losses on top.
